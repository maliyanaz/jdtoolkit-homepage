About Me

My name is Maliya. Currently, I am studying political science and creative coding. Political activism and writing is something I hope I can connect with my computer science interests in the future.

My inspiration stems from Angela Davis and other women leaders like Benazir Bhutto and Tarja Halonen. These women encourage and motivate me everyday that we still have a lot to achieve in terms of equality in society. When I read about all of their accomplishments and what they have done for human rights, I think to myself that I want to make a similar change after higher education someday.
